# Posting Schedule

1 video per channel per week. Minimum 6-day gap between uploads per channel.
Post 1-2 hours before peak viewing so YouTube has time to process, index, and start recommending.

All times are **US Eastern (EST/EDT)**.

## General Rules

- **Consistency > perfect timing.** Pick a day/time per channel and stick to it. YouTube rewards regularity.
- **Post before peak, not during.** YouTube needs 1-2 hours to process and index.
- **Weekday sweet spot:** 2–4 PM EST for most content types.
- **Weekend sweet spot:** 9–11 AM EST (people browse earlier on days off).
- **Worst times:** Late night (11 PM–6 AM) and early Monday morning.
- **Thursday & Friday** are generally the highest engagement days across YouTube.

## Shorts

Shorts follow different rules — they're algorithmically pushed more than pulled by search:
- Post **any time** — timing matters less for Shorts since they're feed-driven.
- If anything, early afternoon (12–3 PM EST) gives a slight edge.
- Volume and consistency matter more than timing for Shorts.
